translations for Alfred in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"Yes, Alfred Varin. He came to see me once on some matter of business....I forget what it was." — Oui, Alfred Varin… Son frère est venu autrefois me solliciter… je ne sais plus à quel propos…
The Extraordinary Adventures of Arsene Lupin, Gentleman Burglar, by Maurice Leblanc Arsène Lupin gentleman-cambrioleur, de Maurice Leblanc
Some one entered. It was Alfred Varin. I recognized him at once, owing to the close resemblance he bore to his brother Etienne. There was the same slouching gait; the same cadaverous face covered with a black beard. Quelqu’un entra. Et je reconnus tout de suite, à sa grande ressemblance avec Étienne Varin, son frère Alfred. Même démarche lourde, même visage terreux envahi par la barbe.
The Extraordinary Adventures of Arsene Lupin, Gentleman Burglar, by Maurice Leblanc Arsène Lupin gentleman-cambrioleur, de Maurice Leblanc
"He has not seen them, but Alfred Varin has told him of their existence and threatened to publish them if my husband should take any steps against him. My husband was afraid....of a scandal." — Il ne les a pas vues, mais Alfred Varin lui en a révélé l’existence, le menaçant de les publier si mon mari agissait contre eux. Mon mari a eu peur… il a reculé devant le scandale.
The Extraordinary Adventures of Arsene Lupin, Gentleman Burglar, by Maurice Leblanc Arsène Lupin gentleman-cambrioleur, de Maurice Leblanc
"I think so; but I do not know. You see, after that last interview with Alfred Varin, and after some harsh words between me and my husband in which he called me to account—we live as strangers." — Il a tout mis en œuvre… du moins, je le suppose, car, à partir de cette dernière entrevue avec Alfred Varin, et après les quelques mots très violents par lesquels il m’en rendit compte, il n’y a plus eu entre mon mari et moi aucune intimité, aucune confiance. Nous vivons comme deux étrangers.
The Extraordinary Adventures of Arsene Lupin, Gentleman Burglar, by Maurice Leblanc Arsène Lupin gentleman-cambrioleur, de Maurice Leblanc
