translations for Harry in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"I don't care two coppers who he is," cried Silver, "but he hasn't paid his score. Harry, run and catch him." — Je ne donnerais pas deux liards pour savoir qui c’est, proclama Silver ; mais il part sans payer. Harry, cours après et ramène-le.
"So?" cried Silver. "In my house! Ben, run and help Harry. One of those swabs, was he? Was that you drinking with him, Morgan? Step up here." — Hein ? Dans ma maison ! Ben, cours prêter main-forte à Harry. Lui, un de ces sagouins ?… Morgan, c’est vous qui buviez avec lui ? Venez ici.
