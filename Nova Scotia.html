translations for Nova Scotia in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"I have been a governess for five years," said she, "in the family of Colonel Spence Munro, but two months ago the colonel received an appointment at Halifax, in Nova Scotia, and took his children over to America with him, so that I found myself without a situation. I advertised, and I answered advertisements, but without success. At last the little money which I had saved began to run short, and I was at my wits' end as to what I should do. — J’ai été gouvernante pendant cinq ans, dit-elle, dans la famille du colonel Spence Munro, mais il y a deux mois le colonel fut envoyé à Halifax, dans la Nouvelle-Écosse, et comme il emmena ses enfants avec lui en Amérique, je me trouvai sans situation. Je mis des annonces dans les journaux, je répondis à celles qui pouvaient me convenir, mais sans succès aucun, si bien que mes faibles ressources commençant à s’épuiser je ne savais réellement plus que devenir.
