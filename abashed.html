translations for abashed in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
He could hardly believe his ears. Suddenly, he grasped the meaning of Angélique's whole behaviour. In front of that sad, plain, but wonderfully gentle face, he stood for a moment discountenanced, almost abashed. He no longer thought of laughing. A feeling of respect, mingled with remorse and kindness, overcame him. Il fut stupéfait, et il comprit soudain toute la conduite d’Angélique. Devant ce visage mélancolique, disgracieux, mais d’une telle douceur, il resta un moment décontenancé, presque confus. Il ne pensait plus à rire. Un sentiment de respect, où il y avait des remords et de la bonté, pénétrait en lui.
