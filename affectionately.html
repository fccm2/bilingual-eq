translations for affectionately in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
He welcomed Armand with tears of joy, and clasped my hand affectionately. I was not long in seeing that the paternal sentiment was that which dominated all others in his mind. Il accueillit Armand avec des larmes de bonheur, et me serra affectueusement la main. Je m'aperçus bientôt que le sentiment paternel était celui qui dominait tous les autres chez le receveur.
He kissed her affectionately and went away. His wife threw him a few kisses and waved her handkerchief. The whistle sounded, and the train started. Il l’embrassa affectueusement, et s’en alla. Sa femme lui envoya par la fenêtre de petits baisers discrets, et agita son mouchoir. Mais un coup de sifflet retentit. Le train s’ébranla.
