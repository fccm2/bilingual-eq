I entered the club. It was just after eleven, and the big room was fairly full, though the rush had not yet set in. I noticed a tall, thin, angular man seated in an arm-chair by the fire. He turned as I drew my chair up to him. It was the man of all others whom I should have chosen—Tarp Henry, of the staff of Nature , a thin, dry, leathery creature, who was full, to those who knew him, of kindly humanity. I plunged instantly into my subject. J’entrai au Club. Onze heures sonnaient. La grande salle commençait à se remplir.
