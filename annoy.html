translations for annoy in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"It is very hot," said Athos, drawing his sword in its turn, "and yet I cannot take off my doublet; for I just now felt my wound begin to bleed again, and I should not like to annoy Monsieur with the sight of blood which he has not drawn from me himself." — Il fait très chaud, dit Athos en tirant son épée à son tour, et cependant je ne saurais ôter mon pourpoint ; car, tout à l’heure encore, j’ai senti que ma blessure saignait, et je craindrais de gêner monsieur en lui faisant voir du sang qu’il ne m’aurait pas tiré lui-même.
