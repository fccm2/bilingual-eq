Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
“Say that she is to be admitted as soon as she comes. But first put out the lamp in the drawing-room, and if anyone comes, say that I have not come back and shall not be coming back.” —Va dire que dès qu'elle rentrera elle vienne. Auparavant, éteins la lampe du salon, et, s'il vient quelqu'un, réponds que je ne suis pas rentrée et que je ne rentrerai pas.
