translations for applause in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
The ballet lasted an hour, and had sixteen ENTREES. The ballet ended amid the applause of the whole assemblage, and everyone reconducted his lady to her place; but the king took advantage of the privilege he had of leaving his lady, to advance eagerly toward the queen. Le ballet dura une heure ; il avait seize entrées. Le ballet fini au milieu des applaudissements de toute la salle, chacun reconduisit sa dame à sa place ; mais le roi profita du privilége qu’il avait de laisser la sienne où il se trouvait pour s’avancer vivement vers la reine.
The little red warriors hung upon the words of the speaker, and when he had finished they burst into a roar of applause, waving their rude weapons in the air. The old chief stepped forward to us, and asked us some questions, pointing at the same time to the woods. Lord John made a sign to him that he should wait for an answer and then he turned to us. Les petits guerriers rouges buvaient les paroles de l’orateur. Quand il eut terminé, ils éclatèrent en applaudissements, brandirent leurs armes grossières.
