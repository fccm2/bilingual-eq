translations for aunt in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"In that case, I suppose my aunt will consent. . . Pray come in." « En ce cas, je suppose que ma tante consentira… Prenez la peine d’entrer. »
"I don't think so, aunt. It's a small blade and I didn't strike him too hard." — Je ne crois pas, ma tante. La lame est fine, et j’ai retenu mon coup. »
Such are the true facts of the death of Dr. Grimesby Roylott, of Stoke Moran. It is not necessary that I should prolong a narrative which has already run to too great a length, by telling how we broke the sad news to the terrified girl, how we conveyed her by the morning train to the care of her good aunt at Harrow, of how the slow process of official inquiry came to the conclusion that the doctor met his fate while indiscreetly playing with a dangerous pet. The little which I had yet to learn of the case was told me by Sherlock Holmes as we travelled back next day. C’est ainsi que mourut le docteur Grimesby Roylott, de Stoke Moran. Il n’est pas nécessaire de prolonger un récit déjà trop long, en racontant comment après avoir appris la vérité à la jeune fille, nous l’amenâmes par le train du matin à sa chère tante de Harrow. L’enquête officielle prouva que le docteur avait trouvé la mort en jouant imprudemment avec un dangereux reptile. Holmes acheva de m’éclairer sur cette sinistre affaire en rentrant à Londres, le lendemain.
His aunt and he stood motionless, for a moment, and confused: Sa tante et lui demeurèrent un moment immobiles, confondus.
"And you who smashed the window-pane while your aunt was throttling me?" — Et c’est vous qui avez brisé la vitre au moment où votre tante m’étranglait ?
"Yes, aunt." — Oui, ma tante.
"Yes, aunt," said a youth standing beside her. — Oui, ma tante, fit un jeune homme qui se tenait à ses côtés. »
