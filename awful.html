translations for awful in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
The old fellow's fury was awful. He sprung to his feet, drew and opened a sailor's clasp-knife, and balancing it open on the palm of his hand, threatened to pin the doctor to the wall. La fureur du vieux drôle fut terrible. Il se dressa d’un bond, tira un coutelas de marin qu’il ouvrit, et le balançant sur la main ouverte, s’apprêta à clouer au mur le docteur.
"My name," said he, "is John Openshaw, but my own affairs have, as far as I can understand it, little to do with this awful business. It is an hereditary matter; so in order to give you an idea of the facts, I must go back to the commencement of the affair. — Mon nom est John Openshaw, mais ma propre histoire a peu de rapport avec cette horrible affaire. C’est un cas d’hérédité que je vais vous exposer et afin que vous le compreniez bien je suis forcé de remonter à plusieurs années en arrière.
