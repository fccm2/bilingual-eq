translations for bad luck in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
“There now!” said Holmes, bitterly, as he emerged panting and white with vexation from the tide of vehicles. “Was ever such bad luck and such bad management, too? Watson, Watson, if you are an honest man you will record this also and set it against my successes!” « Sapristi ! dit Holmes, avec amertume, en se dégageant tout haletant des files de voitures, quelle malchance et aussi quelle imprévoyance de ma part ! Si vous êtes juste, Watson, vous enregistrerez cet échec à mon passif. »
