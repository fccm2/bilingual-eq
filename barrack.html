translations for barrack in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
Each Musketeer was accustomed to leave at the general hotel, as at a barrack, his own horse and that of his lackey. Planchet, Grimaud, Mousqueton, and Bazin set off at full speed. En effet, chaque mousquetaire laissait à l’hôtel général comme à une caserne son cheval et celui de son laquais. Planchet, Grimaud, Mousqueton et Bazin partirent en toute hâte.
