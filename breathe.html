translations for breathe in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
The captain made us trim the boat, and we got her to lie a little more evenly. All the same, we were afraid to breathe. Le capitaine nous fit arrimer le canot, et nous réussîmes à l’équilibrer un peu mieux. Malgré cela, nous osions à peine respirer.
There has been someone to watch by me every night; I can not breathe. What remains of my poor existence is shared between being delirious and coughing. «On m'a veillée toutes les nuits. Je ne pouvais plus respirer. Le délire et la toux se partageaient le reste de ma pauvre existence.
“I wish you wouldn’t squeeze so.” said the Dormouse, who was sitting next to her. “I can hardly breathe.” “Ne poussez donc pas comme ça,” dit le Loir; “je puis à peine respirer.”
