"The arms are easy found, in the sandhill, N. point of north inlet cape, bearing E. and a quarter N. "J. F." That was all, but brief as it was, and, to me, incomprehensible, it filled the squire and Doctor Livesey with delight. « On trouvera sans peine les armes, dans la dune de sable, à l’extrémité N. du cap de la baie nord, direction E. quart N. « J.
