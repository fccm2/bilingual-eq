translations for carpenter in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"How, in the name of good-fortune, did you know all that, Mr. Holmes?" he asked. "How did you know, for example, that I did manual labor. It's as true as gospel, for I began as a ship's carpenter." — Comment, au nom du ciel ! savez-vous cela, monsieur Holmes ? s’écria-t-il. Qui vous a dit que j’avais travaillé de mes mains ? C’est vrai, ma parole, j’ai été charpentier dans la marine.
