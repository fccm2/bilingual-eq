translations for cocktail in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
"There's nothing in it. I looked at it before," said Lestrade. " 'Oct. 4th, rooms 8 s. , breakfast 2 s. 6 d. , cocktail 1 s. , lunch 2 s. 6 d. , glass sherry, 8 d. ' I see nothing in that." — Je l’ai lue comme vous, dit Lestrade. Cela n’a rien de bien curieux : « 4 octobre, chambre 1 shilling, déjeuner 2 shillings 6 pence, cocktail 1 shilling, 1 verre de sherry, 8 pence ». C’est significatif.
