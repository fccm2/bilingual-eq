translations for contract in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
The notary gave him the look which we give to a person who is not quite right in his head. Then, accepting the situation, he took his pen and drew up a contract on stamped paper, acknowledging the payment of the deposit by Captain Jeanniot and promising him a third of such moneys as he should discover: Le notaire le regarda comme on regarde un monsieur dont la raison n’est pas très saine. Puis, se décidant, il prit la plume et libella, sur papier timbré, un contrat qui mentionnait le dépôt du capitaine en retraite Janniot, et lui garantissait un tiers des sommes par lui découvertes.
