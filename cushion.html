translations for cushion in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
She knelt upon a cushion and prayed, with her head buried between her palpitating arms. Elle s’agenouilla sur un coussin et pria, la tête enfoncée entre ses bras palpitants.
There was a table set out under a tree in front of the house, and the March Hare and the Hatter were having tea at it: a Dormouse was sitting between them, fast asleep, and the other two were using it as a cushion, resting their elbows on it, and talking over its head. “Very uncomfortable for the Dormouse,” thought Alice; “only, as it’s asleep, I suppose it doesn’t mind.” Il y avait une table servie sous un arbre devant la maison, et le Lièvre y prenait le thé avec le Chapelier. Un Loir profondément endormi était assis entre les deux autres qui s’en servaient comme d’un coussin, le coude appuyé sur lui et causant par-dessus sa tête. “Bien gênant pour le Loir,” pensa Alice. “Mais comme il est endormi je suppose que cela lui est égal.”
