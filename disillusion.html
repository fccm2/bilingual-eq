translations for disillusion in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
At four o'clock, I telegraphed to my friends at Rouen that an unexpected event would prevent me from making my promised visit. Between ourselves, considering what my friends must now know, my visit is postponed indefinitely. A cruel disillusion for them! À quatre heures je télégraphiais à mes amis de Rouen qu’un incident imprévu me contraignait à remettre ma visite. Entre nous, je crains fort, étant donné ce qu’ils doivent savoir maintenant, d’être obligé de la remettre indéfiniment. Cruelle désillusion pour eux !
