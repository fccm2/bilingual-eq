translations for entrance in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
M. de Treville made his entrance triumphantly into the Fort l'Eveque, whence he delivered the Musketeer, whose peaceful indifference had not for a moment abandoned him. M. de Tréville fit triomphalement son entrée au Fort-l’Évêque, où il délivra le mousquetaire, que sa paisible indifférence n’avait pas abandonné.
"I believe you told me, Monsieur le Baron, that this tunnel is the only subterranean entrance to the castle and that it has been closed up for time immemorial?” — Vous m’avez bien dit, monsieur le baron, que ce puits était l’unique entrée des souterrains, et que, de mémoire d’homme, elle est bouchée ?
