And at this there came suddenly a lowering shadow over his face and he tightened his grasp upon my hand and raised a forefinger threateningly before my eyes. "Now, Jim, you tell me true; that ain't Flint's ship?" he asked. Mais à ces mots une ombre soucieuse envahit tout à coup ses traits. Have no hesitation in lighting one.” « Vous êtes un enthousiaste de votre profession, comme je le suis de la mienne, dit-il.
