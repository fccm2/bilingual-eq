translations for forty-six in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
The dear friend had no opinion; yet he advised the sacrifice of the annuities. Then she withdrew another package and, from it, she took a paper at random. It proved to be a three-per-cent annuity worth two thousand francs. Ludovic placed the package of bonds in his pocket. That afternoon, accompanied by his secretary, he sold the annuities to a stock-broker and realized forty-six thousand francs. Le cher ami n’avait aucune opinion, cependant il conseilla le sacrifice de la Rente. Alors elle prit une autre liasse, et, dans cette liasse, au hasard, un papier. C’était un titre 3% de 1.374 francs. Ludovic le mit dans sa poche. L’après-midi, accompagné de son secrétaire, il fit vendre ce titre par un agent de change et toucha quarante-six mille francs.
