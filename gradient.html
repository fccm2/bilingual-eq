translations for gradient in French

Here are paragraphs from public domain books translated:
In vain we dragged out some of the pieces. The only effect was that the larger ones became detached and threatened to roll down the gradient and crush us. It was evident that the obstacle was far beyond any efforts which we could make to remove it. The road by which Maple White had ascended was no longer available. Nous écartâmes quelques-uns des blocs, sans autre résultat que de nous exposer à l’écrasement, car les plus gros, n’étant plus soutenus, menaçaient de dégringoler la pente. Évidemment, l’obstacle braverait tous les efforts. Le chemin par où avait passé Maple White n’était plus libre.
